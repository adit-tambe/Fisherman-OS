THE PROBLEM

4 Million Fishermen Make
Life-or-Death Decisions Blindly

⛈️

Dangerous Weather Decisions

775 fishermen killed and 113 went missing in Kerala alone (2011-2024). Cyclone Ockhi killed 159 in a single event. Most had no warning.

775 Lives lost in one state
⛽

Fuel Burned on Blind Scouting

Diesel accounts for 60-70% of trip costs. Fishermen spend 2-3 hours "scouting" — burning ₹1,500-3,000 on zero-catch days just looking for fish.

70% of costs = diesel fuel
📉

Price Exploitation by Middlemen

Zero price transparency. Fishermen sell at whatever the first buyer offers. One market pays ₹60/kg while another pays ₹120/kg for the same fish, same morning.

40% income lost to info asymmetry
📱

Their "Tool" Today: WhatsApp Forwards

Decision-making relies on group chat rumors, personal intuition, and Windy.com (which they can't read). No structured intelligence exists.

97.8% don't use any fishing app
"The ocean is hyperlocal. The forecasting isn't."
